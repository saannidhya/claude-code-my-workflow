## Current Claim

The paper should not ask for the causal effect of paying cash as if `cash` were one treatment. The contribution is to show that the mortgage-cash premium bundles multiple mechanisms: ordinary seller-certainty discounts, distress liquidation, corporate/entity acquisition, and investor intermediation.

## Evidence Already In Hand

1. National CoreLogic transaction audit, 2007-2024.
2. Within-cell cash/mortgage decomposition, 2018-2024.
3. Buyer-type decomposition showing ordinary cash, corporate cash, investor cash, and distress cash have very different gaps.
4. Repeat-sale scan with 5.2 million consecutive same-parcel purchase-resale pairs.
